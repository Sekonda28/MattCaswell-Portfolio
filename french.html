À propos de moi

My background is in business analysis and project management, working primarily in the UK public sector (with 2 year a stint in Japan) to deliver a mix of technology and service improvement projects. During the Covid pandemic I started to explore the world of coding and computer programming and quickly developed a passion and the wild thought that I could do this as a career! I have now relocated to France with my family and am officially embarking on a new career in software development.

I am currently self-teaching through the Codecademy Full Stack Development course, FreeCodeCamp, and the Angela Yu Web Development course. I will also be enrolling in a Bootcamp here in France during the Autumn to futher develop my skills. In addition to basic HTML, CSS and Javascript, my key focus at present is on learning the MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, Node). If you have any projects you need help with feel free to get in touch through the contact form below!
